Core Technologies of Artificial Intelligence

The development of AI relies on several key technologies:

Machine Learning (ML)

Enables systems to automatically improve through experience.

Deep Learning (DL)

Mimics neural networks of the human brain to solve complex problems.

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Helps computers understand and generate human language.

Computer Vision (CV)

Allows machines to “see” and interpret images and videos.
