Definition and Development of AI

The concept of artificial intelligence dates back to the 1956 Dartmouth Conference, where scientists proposed that:

“If human thinking can be precisely described, then machines can be made to simulate it.”

Today, AI has been widely applied in speech recognition, image analysis, natural language processing, and autonomous driving.

Main Types of AI

  • Narrow AI (Weak AI) — Focused on specific tasks, such as Siri or ChatGPT.
  • General AI (Strong AI) — Can perform any intellectual task a human can.
  • Superintelligence AI — A theoretical form that surpasses human intelligence.
